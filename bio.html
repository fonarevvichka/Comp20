Welcome to Vichka's Bio

Vichka was born in Lexington Massachusetts. He lived there from 2000 until July of 2018 when he moved South Boston. He went to Lexington Highschool and particiapted heavily in the robotics club. As part of the team "Two Bits and a Byte" he went to the world champinship all four years of highschool. Junior and senior year he was lead programmer and drove the robot. Junior year the team was ranked 3rd in their division at the world championship and was knocked out in the quarter finals. Senior year we partnered with another team from Lexington and won the world championship. 2018 was the year that Vichka got accepted and began studying at Tufts University.
